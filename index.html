About West Coast Jubilee Life Ministries

We are a Christ-centered church serving the Saldanha community with a heart for worship, discipleship, prayer, and meaningful connection. Our desire is to build lives, strengthen families, and share the love of God with everyone who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To Build • To Influence • To Connect

Our Mission

To minister with love, preach the Word faithfully, and help people grow in their relationship with God.

Our Community

A welcoming church home where children, youth, adults, and families can grow together in faith.

Our Worship

Spirit-filled gatherings focused on prayer, biblical teaching, praise, and encouragement for everyday life.
